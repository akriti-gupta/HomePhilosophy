ABOUT THE SERVICE
A First Look is a customized concept board with real life items that suit your space, style and fit your budget. It provides you a 2D insight into how the space will look with all individual items together, all measured to fit accurately into your home. You can view these boards at anytime via your account on our website.
We understand the importance of seeing furniture in real, before buying; after all no two butts feel the same on a sofa! With our curated shopping list you simply go to the stores, make sure you love our picks, and buy only what you want.

No more confusing trips pouring over your floor plans and the furniture measurements!
Home Philosophy does not work with preferred vendors- we source pieces from all over town. Right from Ikea to local shops and online retailers; our goal is to tirelessly scour the city for the best furniture to fit your style and budget.
