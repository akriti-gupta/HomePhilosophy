Our home philosophy is simple: a beautifully designed space changes the way you feel.

Designing a home should be convenient and affordable. No one wants their entire home from the same store, but furniture hopping around town is physically taxing, emotionally draining and downright confusing.

We’re here to change that for you!

Welcome to Home Philosophy. We’re a team of experienced designers who create original and inspiring rooms to transform your house into a home you love. We provide complete interior design services, with a passion for curating the best furniture pieces from around Singapore within your budget.

Let us show you how great design changes everything

The Home Philosophy Experience

Whether providing curated furniture to transform a room or renovating your entire place, we create a home you love.

1
Find your style
With a plethora of styles out there, let us make it simple to find yours! Our effortless Style Quiz helps you identify the best look for your space. As your design partner, we eliminate hours of confusion by showing you amazing room inspirations.
2
We get to know you and your space
Through our fun and Collaborative Online Process we design your dream space! Share all the details you want in advance, your budget, and schedule a meet and measure with us (Yes! We are real people and we want to meet you).
3
Receive your newly designed space
You get several custom Concept Boards to review at your own convenience. Once you have decided what you love and don’t, we’ll refine it. We work together to complete the look so that the design is exactly what you had in mind.
4
Approve your final look and shop with ease
Receive your Final Look, layout plan and complete Shopping List - giving you the flexibility to view the items before buying. Or simply click our 'Buy for me' option at checkout to have us buy and deliver for you.

Doing a full renovation? Let's chat over coffee for us to understand your ideas and provide a custom quote
